subroutine msgini (   LUN)

THIS SUBROUTINE INITIALIZES, WITHIN THE INTERNAL ARRAYS, A NEW BUFR MESSAGE FOR OUTPUT.

ARRAYS ARE FILLED IN COMMON BLOCKS /MSGPTR/ AND MODULES MSGCWD AND BITBUF.
